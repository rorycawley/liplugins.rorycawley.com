Why use the LI Plugins?

  • Drives user engagement
  • Maximises ROI
  • Provides productivity improvements

LinkedIn Plugins Terms

  • By obtaining and using the LinkedIn Plugins you agree to the Plugins Terms of Use!

Enable your page for plugins

  1. Go to developer.linkedin.com to get your API key
  2. Then add the script below to your page replacing the api_key with your own one 
<!doctype html>
<meta charset=utf-8>
<script src=http://platform.linkedin.com/in.js>
    	api_key: 1jn6si63x4
</script>
<title>LinkedIn Says - Members first!</title>

<p>I'm all set to use the LinkedIn Plugins now - let's do this...

Share Button

  • Enable users to share your website with LinkedIn's professional audience
  • Drive traffic back to your site 
<script type="IN/Share" data-counter="right"></script>

Live Example Share Button: Microsoft

Live Example Share Button: Disney

Follow Company Button

  • Make it easy for professionals to engage with your company 
<script type="IN/FollowCompany" data-id="1337" 
	data-counter="right">
</script>

Live Example Follow Company Button: Kiva

Member Profile Plugin

  • Enable LinkedIn members to discover common professional connections 
<script type="IN/MemberProfile" 
	data-id="http://www.linkedin.com/in/rorycawley" 
	data-format="inline" data-related="false">
</script>

Live Example Member Profile: Memsql

Sign-in with LinkedIn

  • Enable users to login/register using their LinkedIn identity 
<script type="in/Login">
	Hello, <?js= firstName ?> <?js= lastName ?>.
</script>

Live Example Member Profile: Emtec

Company Insider plugin

  • Shows users who in their network works at a particular company, the list of new hires and job changes at the company
  • Allow users to follow your company news

Company Insider plugin (code)

<script type="IN/CompanyInsider" data-id="1337" 
	data-modules="innetwork,newhires,jobchanges">
</script>

Live Example Company Insider: Ciena

Company profile plugin

  • Displays key company information at-a-glance
  • Help users discover who they know at the company 
<script type="IN/CompanyProfile" data-id="1337" 
	data-format="inline" data-related="false">
</script>

Live Example Company profile: Wallmart

Apply with LinkedIn plugin

  • Allows candidates to apply for your jobs using their LinkedIn profiles
  • Integrate with your Applicant Tracking System 
<script type="IN/Apply" data-companyid="1337" 
	data-jobtitle="Technical Product Consultant" 
	data-joblocation="ireland" data-phone="required" 
	data-email="rcawley@linkedin.com">
</script>

Live Example Apply With LinkedIn: Netflix

Recommend with LinkedIn

  • Enables users to recommend products and services on LinkedIn
  • Drives traffic to your site 
<script type="IN/RecommendProduct" data-company="LinkedIn" 
	data-product="201714" data-counter="right"> 
</script>

Live Example Recommend with LinkedIn: Cathay Pacific
